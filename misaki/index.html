About Us

           Welcome to Misaki's Handmade Crochet, where creativity and passion intertwine to bring you unique and beautifully handcrafted crochet creations.

         Our story begins with a 19 year old student pursuing a degree in BSIT, deeply in love with the world of arts. In 2021, a thoughtful gift from her best friend, a 50g yarn and a crochet hook, sparked a new and exciting chapter in her life. Little did she know, this simple gift would become the catalyst for her incredible journey in the world of crochet.

Her very first creation was a frog headband, a piece that showed promise but carried a few imperfections. As time went by, she found solace and happiness in crocheting, which soon became her favorite pastime. Encouragement from her family paved the way for her to share her remarkable crochet products with the world. In 2022, she decided to take the plunge and start selling her creations. Initially, her offerings were limited to bags and hats, and while they were meticulously crafted, selling them proved to be a challenging endeavor. Self-doubt crept in, and she questioned whether her creations were truly worth purchasing. This momentary setback led her to take a break from selling but did not deter her from the art of crochet.

In 2023, she mustered the courage to re-enter the world of selling, this time with a new product line - crochet flowers. The timing couldn't have been better, as Valentine's Day was just around the corner. This decision opened the floodgates to a wave of orders and overwhelming support. It was this outpouring of love that rekindled her motivation and inspired her to continue sharing her craft with the world.

The name "Misaki" was inspired by the Japanese word meaning "beautiful flower bloom." Just like a beautiful flower, Misaki's creations are crafted with precision and love, symbolizing the beauty that emerges from dedication and creativity. Misaki's Handmade Crochet now offers a diverse range of crochet creations, including flowers, keychains, and custom-made pieces. Every item is crafted with love and care, a testament to the unwavering dedication and passion of its founder. Misaki's Handmade Crochet is not just a business; it's a journey of growth, creativity, and the joy of sharing handmade artistry with others.

          We invite you to explore our creations and join us on this enchanting journey through the world of crochet. Thank you for being a part of our story, and we look forward to crafting beautiful memories with you.
